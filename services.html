Web and app design + development

The following describes a loose outline around the process used to create web and mobile apps. It'd advised that clients invest in the complete process as they result in a more complete and considered design and engineered solution.

Interaction

This stage is used to understand and define the business and user requirements of the product. These insights are used to frame and rationalise design decisions later in the process.

Visual

Under the constraints framed in the interaction, the visual stage is a chance to explore various visual and communication design solutions. This is often done alongside branding or artwork.

Code

Programming the front-end and back-end of a website or app gives us a chance to really explore in the 2 previous stages. We offer coding services to strenghen our design abilities, being aware of the technological constraints allows us to create original designs.

Web mini-sites

These are small websites with simple interaction requirements (sell new EP / showcase new season / advertise event etc). They involve a heavy focus in art direction, often involving interactive elements, with the goal to create an experience the user won't forget.
